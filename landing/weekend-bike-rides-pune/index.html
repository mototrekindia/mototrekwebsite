Planning Weekend Bike Rides in Pune

Weekend rides are all about taking a break from routine without complicated planning.

You don’t need a long trip or heavy preparation. Just a good route, basic gear and a few hours of free time.

That’s why weekend bike rides in Pune are so popular. Within a short distance, you can experience smooth roads, greenery and open highways.

Best Weekend Bike Ride Options Near Pune

Pune to Lonavala
One of the easiest and most popular rides. Good roads, scenic views and plenty of stops. Perfect for quick morning rides.

Pune to Mulshi
A calm and relaxed route with less traffic. Ideal if you want a peaceful ride.

Pune to Lavasa
A slightly longer route with curves and open roads. Good for riders who want a mix of comfort and fun.

Pune to Tamhini Ghat
Best for riders who enjoy nature and longer rides. Especially good during monsoon.

How to Choose the Right Weekend Ride

Short Time Available
Choose Lonavala or nearby short routes.

Half Day Ride
Mulshi or Lavasa works well.

Full Day Ride
Tamhini Ghat or extended routes.

Choose based on your time and comfort.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which are the best weekend bike rides near Pune?

Lonavala, Mulshi, Lavasa and Tamhini Ghat are among the most popular options.

Are weekend rides suitable for beginners?

Yes, routes like Lonavala are beginner friendly and easy to manage.

How early should I start a weekend ride?

Most riders prefer starting early morning to avoid traffic and enjoy better roads.

What should I carry for a weekend ride?

Helmet, gloves, water and basic essentials are usually enough for short rides.

Is it safe to go on weekend rides alone?

Yes, but it is better to inform someone about your route. Group rides are safer for beginners.
